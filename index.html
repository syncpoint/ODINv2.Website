Built for the Field

Everything you need for tactical planning and coordination, designed to work in challenging environments.

MIL-STD-2525C Symbology

Full support for military standard symbols. Drag, drop, and position tactical graphics with intuitive controls.

Offline-First

Self-host your map tiles and search services. ODIN works reliably without an internet connection.

Real-Time Collaboration

Built on the Matrix ecosystem for distributed, federated coordination across teams and locations.

Flexible Data Sources

Import WMTS, WMS, and GeoJSON layers. Export your work in standard formats for interoperability.

Productive Editing

Copy/paste, undo/redo, and tags for organizing layers. Link features to external URLs to extend your data without complexity.

Cross-Platform

Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Use it on the hardware you already have.

Why Choose ODIN?

In a world of expensive, proprietary C2 systems with vendor lock-in, ODIN offers a different path.

  • Full Transparency Open source under AGPL-3.0. Audit the code, understand what runs on your systems.
  • No Vendor Lock-In Your data stays yours. Export everything in standard formats anytime.
  • No Registration Required Download and start using ODIN immediately. No accounts, no tracking.
  • Self-Hosted Infrastructure Run your own map servers and services. True operational independence.
  • Community Driven Developed with input from real users. Feature requests and contributions welcome.
